05

Key Insights

Derived from dataset analysis

🚀 ML Engineer
Highest Paying Role
ML Engineers and Cloud Engineers command the highest average salaries, often exceeding ₹9–12 LPA even at fresher level due to niche skill demand.
💡 Python & AWS
Most In-Demand Skills
Python leads across Data, ML, and Backend roles. AWS dominates in DevOps and Cloud. React is the top skill for frontend and full-stack positions.
🏢 Bangalore
Tech Capital of India
Bangalore consistently leads in fresher job openings, followed by Hyderabad and Pune. These 3 cities account for over 50% of all postings.
📈 +12%
Internship Advantage
Freshers with prior internship experience have a significantly higher offer rate compared to those without. Internship acts as a key differentiator in competitive hiring processes.
